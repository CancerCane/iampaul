My STORY

Self improvement, Self Help, Self motivating. Self-less

Early retirement loomed by way of a debilitating disability that has effected me 30 years. Not wanting to suffer like many of my brethren of the Armed forces. I needed an avenue to try to keep my mind moving .I decided to teach myself Linux. Which quickly grew into HTML and how the world wide web worked. A duanting journey to keep it mild. More About Me Quite the distance from mastery, I decided to reach out to fellow sole-pros. with some help on getting a foot hold in the information age. Now in that time, I've found many of my ex-military family were battling with our government and it's many bureaus and lacked guidance.
Having been experiencing hard roads the past 10 years. Wishing I knew anything or anyone to point me in any direction as I felt as if I was standing still in the processes. I felt the desire to make a place that wasn't so commercialized that you had to wait on hold for seemingly simple pushes in the right directions. Here I can be reached personally and I will post experiences and answers to some of those questions that the big corporate 501c's don't care to know the answers to.
